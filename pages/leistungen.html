Profile

»Each new situation requires a new architecture«

Jean Nouvel

Scope of Services

Studio Schels supervises regional and national construction projects and takes over all work phases according to HOAI (1-12) if desired, from the first consultation, basic determination and stocktaking to all (preliminary) design, planning and approval services to construction management and supervision.

Together with the clients and users, new buildings, extensions, conversions, modernizations and repairs are carried out.

Main Emphasis

One focus is on building in stock, preferably listed, where individual solutions for the specific structural and spatial problems are developed in close cooperation with clients and users.

Working Method

The technical side of the planning and the detailed advice play no less a role than the creative design.

Quality and sustainability, combined with cost security and adherence to schedules in a family but professional atmosphere is the basis of every cooperation.

Philosophy

Architecture is viewed as an ongoing process: If changes in the situation by users require a change to a building, the existing building is developed with the courage to change, but with respect for tradition.
